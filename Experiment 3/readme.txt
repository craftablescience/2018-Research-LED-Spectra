A small experiment to make sure that Blue LEDs were varying in their light output.
Also, to check that taking the top half of the spectra would reduce instrument error.
NONBLIND.

--- SPECS ---

10 Blue LEDs were scanned 6 times (the first 3 scans are labeled as "B", the last 3 are labeled as "BB").

--- DETAILS ---

/Raw Data
    Contains raw spectra files (*.csv)
/Processed Data
    Contains the Unscrambler file (project.unsb) as well as the top of the spectra (PCA_Top.csv)
    and an image of the PCA Analysis of that file (PCA_Curve_Top.png)
/Master Files
    masterfile.csv (Combination of all files in "/Raw Data")
